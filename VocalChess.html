Play chess with your voice

Description: in order to move a piece you have to pronounce the coordinates of the starting position followed by the ones of the target position, e.g. "b1 a3".

Right now, the commands are in italian. If you want, you can switch the recognition.lang parameter in cinnamon.js to 'en-US', but it does not work properly.
